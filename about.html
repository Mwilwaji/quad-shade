About Us

Who Are WE

Quad Shades is a Nairobi-based luxury shade company specializing in architecturally designed outdoor structures — from designer pergolas and gazebos to custom car parks and commercial canopies. We combine modern aesthetics with durable engineering to elevate outdoor living and working spaces across Africa.


Founded in 2020 by architect and entrepreneur Armstrong Omondi Mboya, our journey began with one bold idea: to reimagine shade as an experience — not just a structure. Since then, we've completed hundreds of installations across Kenya, Rwanda, and beyond, building a trusted name in high-performance outdoor solutions.

Durable Design

We use weather-resistant materials for reliable, year-round performance.

Affordable Solutions

We offer cost-effective shading without compromising style or quality.

Expert Installation

Our trained team ensures every project meets structural and aesthetic standards.

Our Mission

To deliver sustainable, premium-grade shade systems that redefine outdoor comfort and elevate design standards across Africa.

Our Vision

To become Africa’s most trusted name in luxury outdoor shade — recognized for innovation, integrity, and excellence.

About The Founder

Founder Armstrong Omondi Mboya was named one of Kenya’s Most Promising Founders Under 30 by TheFounder.Africa, recognized for transforming African outdoor spaces with bold, design-led solutions.


"As an architecture student, I saw a gap—stunning homes with uninviting outdoor spaces. Quad Shades was born to change that. Today, our structures don’t just offer shade; they create lifestyle-defining environments."


Armstrong Omondi Mboya, Founder
