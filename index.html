ABOUT US

Quad Shades is a Nairobi-based luxury shade company specializing in architecturally designed outdoor structures; from designer pergolas and gazebos to custom car parks and commercial canopies. We combine modern aesthetics with durable engineering to elevate outdoor living and working spaces across Africa.

WHAT WE OFFER

Pergola

Crafted with premium materials and architectural precision, our pergolas offer comfort, protection, and timeless beauty — elevating outdoor living with refined design.

Canopies

Stylish and practical overhead coverings for doorways, patios, or walkways — adding shelter and visual interest to both modern and traditional exteriors.

Gabezo

Elegant freestanding structures ideal for gardens, lounge areas, and open-air social spaces — where luxury meets lasting functionality.

Sailor Shades

Architectural shade sails made with heat- and weather-resistant fabric — offering graceful, flowing forms that combine minimalist design with maximum sun protection.

Car Covers

Premium car shade systems that shield vehicles from harsh sunlight, dirt, and rain — perfect for private residences, businesses, or public lots.

Car Parking Shades

Engineered to deliver 100% waterproof protection and heat resistance, our car parking shades are built to elevate both form and function in residential and commercial settings.
